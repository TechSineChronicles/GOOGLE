Manufacturing & Supply Chain

Provide the manufacturing capability to deliver Google's leading-edge physical infrastructure.

Google's custom-designed equipment makes up one of the largest and most powerful computing infrastructures in the world. The Supply Chain Operations team is responsible for providing the manufacturing capability to deliver Google's leading-edge physical infrastructure.

As a Manufacturing Engineer, you collaborate with Design Engineers to determine our infrastructure needs and product specifications for a wide variety of designs. You will team with Commodity Managers to select and engage vendors to provide component technologies that enable state-of-the-art warehouse computing. You work side-by-side with our manufacturing partners to create the processes, tools and procedures to manufacture the hardware behind Google’s powerful search technology, ensuring these processes are repeatable and in control. Your work ensures that the various pieces of Google’s infrastructure fit together perfectly and keep our systems humming along smoothly for a seamless user experience.
