Partnerships

Expand our business into new territories.

At Google, we set goals for ourselves we know we can’t reach yet. Our New Business Development and Partnerships teams are made up of technologists, entrepreneurs and leaders who have an eye for what’s next. We develop relationships with a range of organizations, from content providers to publishers, and find ways for them to reach their audiences and manage their digital businesses. Our teams also expand our businesses into new territories, working across Google to develop products and ideas that revolutionize the way people connect with information. The present is just a launch pad for what’s possible.
