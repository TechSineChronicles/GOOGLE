      Rukmini Devi’s 112th Birthday

    • Today’s Doodle celebrates the legacy of Rukmini Devi on what would have been the classical dancer and choreographer’s 112th birthday. Inspired by the bright fabrics and flowing ribbons that accompany Bharata Natyam--a traditional Indian dance form with roots stretching back over a thousand years--the illustration honors Devi’s role in popularizing and spreading the dance all over the world. Originally performed in Hindu temples, Bharata Natyam had almost disappeared until Devi rescued it from obscurity, modernized its style and infused it with elements of the music, dramatic performances and costumes she encountered in her travels.

      Happy birthday, Rukmini.
