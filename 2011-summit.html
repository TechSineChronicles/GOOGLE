European Data Centre Summit 2011

On April 24, 2011, we hosted leaders of the global data center industry in Zurich, Switzerland to share and discuss the latest in data center energy efficiency best practices. We saw case studies from Amazon, Norman Disney and Young, and Telecity Group. We heard about local cooling solutions and geo-independent approaches to efficiency from DeepGreen, IBM, Plusserver, Verne Global, and others. And we held two panel sessions to further discuss best practices and free cooling.
