Another Win for Google

More Aussies and Kiwis dream of working for Google than any other organisation, according to the Insync Surveys and the RedBalloon 2011 Dream Employers Survey. Googler Joe Krayer attributes the big win to our culture and our approach to recognition - we reward our Googlers for being innovative and finding solutions that put our users and business partners first.
