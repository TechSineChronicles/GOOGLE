“Finding creative solutions to energy issues has become a major priority for Google co-founder and CEO Larry Page in recent years…[H]e believes that the corporate world needs to operate more sustainably, and he is determined to build the nation’s first zero-carbon company. This means a business that ultimately is so energy efficient and uses so much clean power that it emits no greenhouse gas—a very tall order indeed. Experts aren’t sure whether it’s even possible for a company to emit no carbon, but Google is trying to come as close to that goal as possible.“
